What
Is Called An Art?

Art is a broad term that encompasses a wide variety of creative activities and objects, including painting, sculpture, music, and dance. Art can be used to express ideas, emotions, and stories.Art is the use of skill and imagination to create aesthetic objects.The definition of art has changed over time and varies across cultures.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film, and Music. However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the Liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.
